As a 2015 High School All-American and 2016 World Rugby U20 Trophy veteran, Ruben de Haas has continued to climb in his rugby career. Serving as vice Captain for the Under-20s in early 2017, de Haas joined the Men’s Selects for the Americas Pacific Challenge later that year where he impressed Eagle coaches.

In 2018, he earned his firs cap with the Men’s Eagles during the Americas Rugby Championship (ARC) against Chile, suiting up for the remaining two matches where he helped the squad go 5-0 and earn their second consecutive ARC title victory.
